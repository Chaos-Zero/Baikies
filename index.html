    Location

    Baikies Chipshop Takeaway is located on the main street of Finstown, a short trip between Stromness and Kirkwall. Why not stop in for your tea on Friday and Saturday nights as we accept preorders and walk-ins.

    We are not affiliated with the Baikies shop, so please do not use their car park.
